ABOUT US

We are a team of web development enthusiasts and students of the National Institute of Technology Durgapur who are pursuing their Bachelors of Technology Degree in various domains. As curious students, we are continuously searching for various opportunities to enhance our skills and benefit society by eradicating their problems.

What matters to us ?

For us, one of the most important things that matter is the problems of the students which we try to solve with our skills and make their lives easier by enlightening their path towards attaining perfection and success in the end besides helping them to explore new ideas and technologies.

What you do ?

We work together to design, create and produce content and ideas that we believe in and that provide a way to benefit the youth of the society.

How you do it ?

We search the internet for hackathons and various softwares and websites that provide resources for our use. Besides this, we grab knowledge about softwares and try to pass on the knowledge to the students in a much easier way.

Come let's dive deep into development
